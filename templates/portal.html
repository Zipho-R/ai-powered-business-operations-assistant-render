Internal Department Access

The Department Portal

Department teams use this portal to access their own ticket queues, review assigned requests, manage workflow progress, add resolution comments, and support internal service operations.

HR Queue

Handles employee-related requests and sensitive HR support tickets.

IT Queue

Receives technical issues such as systems, access, devices, and connectivity.

Finance Queue

Supports payment, approval, invoice, reimbursement, and audit-related tickets.

Operations Queue

Manages business processes, facilities, stock, logistics, and operational workflow requests.

Access Control Note:
Department users only see tickets routed to their selected department. Executive-level dashboards, analytics, and reports are protected separately through admin access.
