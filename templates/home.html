What the platform does

The system combines machine learning classification with rule-based governance to support realistic service desk operations.

  • Predicts the correct ticket department
  • Detects priority and urgency
  • Routes tickets into department workflows
  • Flags sensitive or risky requests
  • Tracks response time after tickets are closed
  • Supports analytics, reports, and forecasting

How the workflow moves

The platform follows a simple operational flow from ticket submission to resolution. Each step is connected so the dashboards, analytics, and reports stay updated.

01

Submit

Users describe their issue through the ticket logging page.

02

Classify

The ML model predicts the department and records the confidence level.

03

Route

The system sends the ticket to the correct department or review queue.

04

Resolve

Departments process the ticket, close it, and generate response-time data.

Executive oversight

Executives can monitor organisation-wide ticket activity, risk flags, approval status, AI confidence, workload trends, and report outputs. The executive view supports visibility and decision-making, not day-to-day ticket handling.

Realistic operations

Departments manage the active work. Tickets move from open to in progress and then closed. Response time is calculated when the ticket is closed, making the workflow more realistic than using random response-time values.
