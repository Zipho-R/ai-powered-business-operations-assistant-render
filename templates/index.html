What happens after submission?

The request enters an automated service workflow. AI classification helps route the ticket, while department staff manage progress and closure.

1

Capture

The system records the requester name, email, and ticket description.

2

Classify

The request is classified into HR, IT, Finance, Operations, or Unclassified.

3

Route

The ticket is routed to the correct department queue or manual review queue.

4

Resolve

Department staff close the ticket with a final resolution comment.

Business Note:
Ticket intake is open for request submission, while internal dashboards, analytics, reports, and department queues remain protected through access-control forms.
